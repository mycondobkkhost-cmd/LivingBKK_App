# PROPPITER — Brand brief (from mockups 2026-06)
# LivingBKK → PROPPITER rebrand | PropTech startup

## Brand name
PROPPITER (all caps in UI; concept: Property + Piter)

## Tagline — Thai (official separator: •)
โพสต์ฟรี • ปิดดีลไว • ไม่ต้องหาลูกค้าเอง

## Tagline — English
Post for free • Close deals faster • No chasing clients yourself

## Typography
- Wordmark EN: geometric sans bold (Prompt SemiBold/Bold or equivalent)
- Tagline TH/EN: Prompt Regular/Medium
- Note: "PROP" solid navy; "PITER" pink/gradient accent on light backgrounds
